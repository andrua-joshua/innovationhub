Who We Are

Empowering Digital Innovation in Uganda

Welcome to the National ICT Innovation Hub, your first destination for fostering homegrown digital solutions and promoting digital employment throughout Uganda.

Situated within the Uganda Institute of Information and Communications Technology (UICT) Nakawa Campus, our hub serves as a dynamic environment for ICT innovators facing challenges with workspace, limited internet connectivity and searching for creative spaces that spur thinking.

Join us at the National ICT Innovation Hub and be part of a vibrant community dedicated to driving digital innovation and economic growth in Uganda.
